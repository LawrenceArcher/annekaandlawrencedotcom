By Car:

Burley Manor is in the heart of Burley village, just 10 minutes off the A31 to Bournemouth. There is plenty of free parking available on the grounds of the venue. Please be aware of free-roaming wild ponies, cattle and pigs within the village!

By Train:

Guests travelling by train from London can take a 1.5hr train from Waterloo to Brockenhurst, and then a 15 minute taxi from Brockenhurst. It is strongly advised to pre-book your taxi as there are not many firms or drivers available in the New Forest, especially off season.
